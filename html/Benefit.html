Exercise & Nutrition planner

Type: Individual work

When: 2024

Duration: Three weeks, half time

Project scope: School project in Graphical Interfaces

The aim of this project was to design a desktop application that supports users’ well-being through personalised fitness and nutrition plans. The design process was iterative, with continuous feedback from teachers and fellow students helping to refine both functionality and visual expression. A Figma prototype was developed to illustrate key features such as customisable workouts, meal plans and progress tracking. To explore different user needs and motivations, two user scenarios, Louise and Victor were created, which represented distinct approaches to fitness and nutrition planning. The overall focus was on usability, clarity and a user-centred experience that feels both supportive and adaptable.

User 1 - Louise

Louise, 36, is new to fitness and nutrition and wants to build a more balanced routine without feeling overwhelmed. Through Benefit, she can easily start her journey by creating a profile with personal and dietary preferences. The platform then generates a ready-made plan that combines workouts and meals designed to fit her lifestyle. Louise appreciates how simple it is to adjust her plan, explore new recipes and stay motivated by tracking her progress visually. In the app, Louise can:

  • Adjust her weekly schedule in the calendar
  • Swap or remove sessions
  • Explore and edit recipes
  • Share grocery lists
  • Track progress and mark workouts as done

User 2 - Victor

Victor, 27, is a dedicated and active user who wants to refresh his training routine. After months of following the same workouts, he returns to Benefit to find new inspiration. Instead of choosing a fixed plan, he decides to generate suggestions based on his updated preferences and allergy information. Victor values flexibility, he prefers to customize his experience, pick exercises that match his goals and save favorites to build his own schedule. In the app, Victor can:

  • Browse and filter workouts
  • Hide calorie information
  • Save favorite exercises and meals
  • Create and send grocery lists
  • Add activities to his personal calendar

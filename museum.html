A helmet

Type: Group work

When: 2020

Duration: Ten weeks, half time

Project scope: School project

The purpose of the project was to design a helmet for the company Styr & Ställ. The product had to match the company's design language and meet identified user requirements. The primary objective was to design a helmet that appeals to a wide range of users, as the most effective helmet is the one that is constantly used.

Process

A mood board was created to establish a shared understanding of the emotions the product should evoke.

Sketches

The result

The curve of the bicycle frame inspires the stripes on the helmet, and the color is chosen to match the bike's color. The soft curves were combined with numerous ventilation holes to obtain a sporty appearance. The helmet has a hard outer shell made of ABS plastic, which is lightweight, water resistant, and has a low production cost. These properties were necessary for the helmet to withstand the many weather conditions in Gothenburg. The helmet's inner shell consists of the shock-absorbing foam material EPP. The helmet contains no soft sections to keep the surface clean and fresh.

A poster

The aim of the poster was to provide a clear and engaging explanation of the products and their connection to the existing Styr & Ställ system. The poster contains a bike path mirroring the curve, drawing inspiration from the bike frame's shape. The color palette was selected to align with Styr & Ställ's website, ensuring consistency with the system's overall graphic profile for easy recognition and association.
